---
nimi-suli: len akesi li seme?
jan-pali: waso Aki
tags:
  - sona
---

*akesi suli liô ken wawa tawa jan.* jan liô lukin eô kiwen moli pi akesi pi tenpo weka mute (Dinosauria) la, ona liô toki insa: "akesi ni liô seme tawa lukin? selo ona liô len seme?" tenpo mute la, sijelo pi akesi moli la, kiwen insa liô awen taso lon tenpo mute suli. 

tenpo pini la, jan sona liô toki: "kiwen moli ni liô sama akesi, la selo liô sama akesi kin! ona liô jo eô nena lili muteô a!"

ni la, jan mute liô sitelen eô akesi kepeken selo ni. taso selo akesi liô awen taso lon kiwen pi mute lili, liô ken suli mute. tenpo sike #MALW la, jan sona liô kama jo eô kiwen akesi pi awen selo. akesi moli pi moku kasi (Dakota, hadrosaur) liô kama kiwen la, kiwen liô awen eô sijelo ona eô selo ona. lupa pakala liô awen kin. nena pi selo ona liô suli, liô lili. 

tenpo #LLLTWAMMMW la, jan sona liô kama jo eô kiwen waso (Archeopteryx). ni liô awen eô kiwen moli, eô len waso. jan mute liô toki: "a! ni liô waso pi tenpo nanpa wan!" jan sona ante liô lukin eô kiwen ni la, ona liô toki: "sijelo pi akesi ante liô ken sama sijelo waso. len pi akesi ni liô ken ala ken sama len waso?"

tenpo sike #LLLTTAMMMMLLLW la, jan sona liô kama jo eô kiwen akesi... pi len waso (Sinosauropteryx). sijelo ona liô sama akesi, taso selo ona liô sama wasoô a! akesi pi sijelo sama waso liô kenô a len sama waso! jan sona liô kama jo eô kiwen mute pi len waso kin. jan sona liô ken lukin eô ijo pi kule len kepeken ilo lukin pi ijo liliô a. ni la, jan liô sona eô kule pi akesi ni. akesi waso pi luka waso tu tu (Microraptor) liô pimeja. akesi Sinosauropteryx liô kule sama kiôÙjeôÙteôÙsanôÙtaôÙkaôÙlu loje. len waso liô ken lon kiwen pi ko kasi kin. tenpo sike #MALLLW la, jan sona Lida Xing liô kama jo eô kiwen pi ko kasi lon ma esun pi ko kasi lon ma Mijama. kiwen pi ko kasi ni liô awen eô linja monsi pi akesi waso, eô len waso!

ni la, jan mute liô sitelen eô akesi ni kepeken len waso. jan sona liô toki: "akesi aleô a pi sijelo sama waso liô len ala len waso?" tenpo ni la, jan sona liô toki: ala. selo akesi liô pana eô seli. akesi suli liô ken len waso lon ma lete tan ni: len liô awen eô seli. taso, akesi suli liô lon ma seli la, len liô jo eô seli muteô a! seli mute liô ken moli eô akesi suli. akesi lili en waso liô ken len waso tan ni: kon liô ken lete eô akesi lili eô waso. taso, akesi lili mute liô ken kama suli. akesi pi ma seli liô kama suli la, len liô weka. taso selo akesi liô ken lon ala anpa pi len waso. selo ona liô len ala, sama jan, sama soweli suli.

tenpo ni la, jan liô sona eô ni: akesi pi tenpo weka mute liô ken sama ijo mute tawa lukin. selo akesi liô ken sama akesi, liô ken len waso, liô ken len ala.
